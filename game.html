How to play

The goal of the game is to collect the most gems by the end of the game. Your collected gems are in your store (the bigger pocket on your right).

When it's your turn, click on one of your pockets to pick the gems inside. You will then drop the gems in the next pockets, one by one, counter-clockwise. You can't drop a gem in the opponent's pocket. There are only two tricks you can do :

Extra turn:
When the last gem you drop is in your pocket, you take another turn.
Capture:
When the last gem you drop is in an empty pocket on your side, you capture this last gem plus all the gems in the pocket in front of it. Captured gems go directly in your store.

The game ends when all six pockets on one side of the board are empty. The player who still has gems on their side of the board captures all of those pieces.
